Lasagna

Description:

A layered pasta dish made with sheets of lasagna noodles, cheese, meat, and sauce, baked until bubbly and golden.

Ingredients

  • ½ pound ground pork
  • ½ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ⅛ teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground pork, ground beef, and onion over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon parsley, garlic, basil, 1 ½ teaspoons salt, oregano, and sugar. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, combine cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, 2 teaspoons salt, and black pepper.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer with noodles, cottage cheese mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, ending with meat sauce and mozzarella cheese on top.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  7. Let it cool for 15 minutes before serving.
